## Mode: TEST ADS (Safe for Development)

Current: `static const bool _useTestAds = true;`

Test ads show "Test Ad" label and never charge real revenue.

---

## To Enable Production Ads

1. Open: `lib/services/ad_service.dart`
2. Change line 11 to: `static const bool _useTestAds = false;`
3. Link app to Play Store in AdMob Console
4. Deploy to Play Store
5. Google reviews (1-3 days)
6. Ads go live automatically
